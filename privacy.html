DailyWins Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2026

Overview

DailyWins is an energy-first daily planner that helps you focus on what matters. We believe your personal data should stay personal. We don't collect any of your data. Everything you enter in DailyWins stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. DailyWins operates entirely offline and does not transmit any data from your device.

Information We Don't Collect

  • No account or login required
  • No email addresses
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reports sent to us
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data shared with third parties
  • No health or fitness data

Data Storage

Your daily wins, energy levels, intentions, and reflections are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all your data is deleted with it.

If you use iCloud backup for your device, your DailyWins data may be included in that backup, subject to Apple's privacy practices.

In-App Purchases

DailyWins offers an optional Pro upgrade. All transactions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Notifications

DailyWins can send you optional morning and evening reminders. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not require any server communication. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in Settings.

Children's Privacy

DailyWins does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, children can safely use DailyWins with the same privacy protections as all users.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect your contact information, we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
