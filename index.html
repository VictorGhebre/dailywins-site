An energy-first daily planner that matches your tasks to how you actually feel. Check your energy, set realistic intentions, and celebrate small wins.

How it works

  • Check your energy level each morning
  • Set 1-3 realistic intentions (based on energy)
  • Complete your wins throughout the day
  • Reflect on what helped or blocked you
  • Fresh start at midnight—no backlog, no guilt
